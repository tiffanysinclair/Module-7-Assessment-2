BBQ fish sauce wings

This gluten-free chicken wings recipe is super easy to make and a real crowd pleaser at any summer barbecue

Prep time: 35 mins

Serves: 4

Steps

  1. Whisk all the marinade ingredients in a bowl until the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Add the chicken wings, coat well and chill for at least 2 hours but preferably overnight.
  3. Add the chicken wings, coat well and chill for at least 2 hours but preferably overnight.
  4. Remove the wings and pat dry with kitchen paper. Add the marinade ingredients to a pan and bring to a boil over a medium heat. Simmer for 5-10 minutes until syrupy.
  5. Heat a barbecue until medium-high (or for oven cooking, see cookâ€™s notes).
  6. Add the wings to a bowl and toss with 1 tbsp of oil and some seasoning.
  7. Grill for 20-25 minutes, turning regularly, until really chargrilled and crisp.
  8. Remove from the barbecue and toss with the reduced marinade. Serve with lime wedges.
